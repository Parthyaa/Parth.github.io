About Me

Hi, I'm Parth Garud — a passionate UI/UX designer driven by curiosity, empathy, and a deep love for clean, functional design. I craft digital experiences that are not just visually appealing but also intuitive, meaningful, and user-centered.

With hands-on expertise in Figma, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Canva, I specialize in translating complex ideas into elegant interfaces and cohesive brand stories. From mobile apps and landing pages to full-fledged design systems, my approach blends strategic UX thinking with a sharp aesthetic sensibility.

What sets me apart is my ability to see design not just as decoration, but as a solution — a way to simplify interactions, solve real user problems, and create emotional connections.
